Nail Care

Do your nails need a special treatment? Find out what these 3 nail care products can do and how to use them.

01 Nail Care Tri-phasic Nail Oil

2B tri-phasic nail oil, thanks to its three phases, is an effective and complete product for nail care. Because of its composition with hydrating oil, like jojoba oil and blackcurrant oil, it is a very good moisturizing and nourishing for nail culticles. Thanks to the presence of linseed oil, with anit-inflammatory properties, and of bamboo, natural hardener, it can be used regularly to re-mineralize damaged nails. The Vitamin E, finally, has an antioxidant effect on cells, slowing down the aging.


02 Nail Care Watergel + Scrub

2B aqua gel + scrub generates hydration and nourishment to nails and cuticles thanks to aloe vera and bamboo. Inside the soft scrubs contained into the product there is vitamin E that is released when the scrub is broken during the massage of the nail and the cuticle. Corundum grains smooth and remove the dead skin from the nails in full compliance with the nail pH. The presence of aloe vera completes the treatment soothing possible cuticle iriitations.


03 Nail Care 9-in-1

2B 9 in 1 nail polish helps your nails to remain strong and healthy, thanks to the combined action of 9 active ingredients. if used frequently you can obtain a herdening, anti-oxidizing, protective from UV rays, trengthening, moisturizing, anti-aging, re-mineralizing, whitening and fungal protection. This product contains some nanoparticles vitamin E, arginine, keratine and tea tree oil. It contains also the Chios mastic gum oil that strengthens your nails and stimulates the production of keratin.
